IVIS is developed by Zhigang Lu, with the power of jQuery and HighCharts. Although it can be used as a general chart editor, it’s more powerful when you need to visualise and analyse large datasets, e.g., RNA-seq data. Please note that this is only for academic use.

If you find it useful for your research, please cite as:

Lu Z & Zhang Y (manuscript in preparation) Facilitated analysis of large data sets by interactive visualisation. https://github.com/zglu/ivis

Please contact me for any issues or development suggestions. Please be aware that questions can only be addressed out of working hours (Mon - Fri 9:00-:17:00 UK Time excluding UK holidays).
